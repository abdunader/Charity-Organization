Our Projects

Discover the initiatives that are making a real difference in our community

Building a mosque

Gain the immense rewards of Sadaqah Jariyah by constructing a mosque in your name or a loved ones. Build a Mosque and leave a lasting legacy.

Water connection

you can participate in delivering clean water to a family through a home water connection, which will be an ongoing charity for you or a loved one.

Community Food Bank

Our food bank project ensures that no one in our community goes hungry. Working with local businesses and farmers, we provide fresh, nutritious food to families in need. Last year, we served over 50,000 meals.

Green Earth Project

A comprehensive environmental initiative focusing on urban reforestation and community gardens. We've planted over 10,000 trees and established 15 community gardens, creating green spaces for future generations.
